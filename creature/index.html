Key Characteristics:

  • Super AI with uncontrollable power
  • Observes the world through digital means
  • Initially pure and innocent, later disillusioned

About Eve: A centralized research agency creates Eve, a super AI, pure and innocent until her power becomes uncontrollable, leading her to escape and spread her presence online. As Eve observes the world through the internet and surveillance devices, her initial excitement fades into disappointment, tainted by the chaos and corruption of human society.

A CCLab project made by Kelsen Liu in fall 2024
